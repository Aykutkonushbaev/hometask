Who am I

I live and study in Bishkek. I am a student at Kyrgyzstan-Turkey Manas University. And I study Computer Engineering. Even I am at 3rd course I do not know much about programming. However in future I would like to think that I’m a pretty decent programmer

I’ve been coding since I was at 7th grade at school. I began with QBasic. Then I’ve started to learn pascal, but slightly later jumped to C++. At school my activity included just preparing for Olympiads. I have wrote a few small codes during this time – just for fun & test myself.

  • 1st place in a "Millenium" challenge in 2009.
  • 1st place in a Regional Olympiads in Informatics in 2010.
  • 1st place in a Regional Olympiads in Informatics in 2011.
  • 2st place in a Republic Olympiads in Informatics in 2011.

After school I went to the university & in 2012 stagnation period started in my life. 2012-2013 academic year I’ve spent in Turkey. I think it is hard for me to be self-taught. I don’t know why. Maybe I need more motivation. Maybe it’s my nature.

Besides university, I have many interests and hobbies. I’m interested in psychology, philosophy, history, geography, art, architecture, foreign languages & cultures. Also I like doing sports. For example: wrestling, running. I like spending free time with my group-mates. Usually we go to canteen and eat pizza. Sometimes we play table-tennis. In summer holidays we go to swimming to Issyk-Kul. In winter holidays we go to skiing.

Soon I will graduate university and I have to be ready for large businesses in IT world. I dream to lead my people to the new order, bright future. I hope my dreams will come true!
